Why We Exist

Core Objectives

ACE Club was founded to close the gap between classroom theory and real-world technical competence — across AI, Cybersecurity, and Embedded Systems.

01 ⚡

Skill Development

Provide practical, hands-on knowledge of AI, cybersecurity, and embedded systems. Improve problem-solving ability, technical depth, and proficiency with industry-standard tools.

02 🔐

Cybersecurity Awareness

Educate students and the campus community on cyber threats, online safety, responsible digital behavior, and the ethics of AI-powered and connected systems.

03 🧪

Practical Learning

Organize workshops, seminars, CTF competitions, AI hackathons, and hardware labs. Hands-on experience over theoretical learning — always.

04 🏆

College Representation

Compete in national and international events, build connections with technical communities, and put Government College of Engineering, Keonjhar on the map.

05 🔗

Interdisciplinary Integration

Explore the intersection of AI, cybersecurity, and embedded hardware — areas like intelligent threat detection, secure IoT design, and real-world system building.

06 🌱

Community Building

Create a collaborative, inclusive technical community within the CSE department where every student — beginner to advanced — has a place to learn and contribute.

Our Vision

Build. Hack. Innovate.

ACE Club was born from the idea that the most impactful technology today sits at the crossroads of intelligence, security, and hardware. We want our members to be ready for a world where these disciplines are inseparable — and we're building that foundation one session at a time.
