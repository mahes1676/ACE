Our Wings

Three Disciplines. One Club.

ACE brings together three of the most critical fields in modern computing. Cybersecurity is our founding and flagship wing.

🛡️

Cybersecurity Flagship Wing

Our founding wing and top priority. Cybersecurity at ACE covers everything from ethical hacking and penetration testing to digital forensics and malware analysis. We train members to think like attackers so they can defend like experts — through CTF competitions, lab sessions, and real-world challenges.

What You'll Learn

  • Ethical Hacking & Penetration Testing
  • Network Security & Packet Analysis
  • Web Application Security (OWASP)
  • Digital Forensics & Incident Response
  • Malware Analysis & Reverse Engineering
  • Cryptography & Steganography
  • CTF (Capture The Flag) Competitions

Tools & Platforms

Kali Linux Burp Suite Metasploit Wireshark Nmap Hack The Box TryHackMe picoCTF OWASP ZAP John the Ripper Ghidra

Artificial Intelligence

The AI wing explores machine learning, deep learning, and intelligent systems. From building predictive models to deploying real-world AI applications — including AI-powered cybersecurity tools — this wing sits at the frontier of what's possible with data and computation.

What You'll Learn

  • Machine Learning Fundamentals
  • Deep Learning & Neural Networks
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Computer Vision
  • AI for Cybersecurity (Threat Detection)
  • Model Training, Evaluation & Deployment
  • AI Ethics & Responsible AI

Tools & Platforms

Python TensorFlow PyTorch Scikit-learn Kaggle Google Colab Hugging Face OpenCV Pandas Matplotlib
💻

Embedded Systems

The Embedded Systems wing bridges the physical and digital worlds. Members design and build hardware-software systems — from secure IoT devices to real-time microcontroller projects. This wing intersects deeply with cybersecurity through secure hardware design and IoT threat modeling.

What You'll Learn

  • Microcontroller Programming (AVR, ARM)
  • IoT Device Design & Protocols
  • Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS)
  • Sensor Integration & Hardware Interfaces
  • Secure IoT & Hardware Hacking
  • Embedded Linux
  • PCB Design Basics

Tools & Platforms

Arduino Raspberry Pi ESP32 STM32 FreeRTOS MQTT Proteus KiCad C/C++ MicroPython
