Our Story

How ACE Began

ACE Club began as a focused Cybersecurity club — a proposal from five second-year CSE students at Government College of Engineering, Keonjhar who wanted hands-on technical experience beyond the classroom.

After evaluation and guidance from the Head of Department, the scope was expanded to include Artificial Intelligence and Embedded Systems alongside Cybersecurity — creating a truly interdisciplinary technical club that reflects the direction modern computing is heading.

The name ACE stands for AI · Cybersecurity · Embedded Systems. Cybersecurity remains our founding and flagship wing. The club operates under the CSE Department with full faculty support and HOD approval.
